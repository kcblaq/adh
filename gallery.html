About us

ADDHF is committed to reaching out to all who are homeless and to provide a shelter for them.

Engaging in random acts of kindness by generally giving aid humanitarian supports to people in need.

ADDHF partners with existing Charity Foundations to enable us share collective ideas on how we can best support and help our various communities.
