Welcome to ADDHF

The Amor De Dios Hope Foundation (ADDHF) is a small, UK/Nigerian-based not-for-profit charity with CAC registration number 115482, Supporting orphans, the elderly, homeless people and widows in Nigeria, the ADDHF invests 100% of its donations into projects that provide homes, food, shelter, education and training to vulnerable individuals.

Our Causes

HELP FOR ORPHANES

ORPHANES

We get children off the streets, providing them with shelter, food, medical care and clothing. Our aim is to give these children prospects in life through education and counselling.
INDEPENDENCE FOR WIDOWS

WIDOWS

Through training programs and with the help of grants and a mentoring system, widowed mothers, left with the no prospects or means to care for their families can have the chance to support themselves and rebuild their lives.
SUPPORTING THE ELDERLY

AGED

We work to ensure there are continual supplies of food via local foodbanks, as well as supporting those individuals who are unable to fend or cook for themselves.
TACKLING HOMELESSNESS

HOMELESS

We provide temporary shelter in the form of sleeping tents, medical care and food to homeless individuals. We are also working to ensure these vulnerable people get permanent homes and the chance to rebuild their lives.

Our Projects

widows empowernment in benin city.

On the 25th of august, 2018, Amor De Dios Hope Foundation Organized an empowerment project to help and support widowed mothers (and a father) with cash grants to kick-start or boost their businesses so as to be more self-reliant and be able to cater for their children.

They were also taught and trained on basic accounting and small business management. Together we can do more.

Hospital visitation.

Amor De Dios Hope Foundation paid a visit to Central Hospital in Benin City on the 15th of July, 2018, on a ‘Random Acts of kindness‘ outreach project.

. There we gave baby care gifts to new mothers that had delivered through CS. We also helped some patients with medical bills who had no money to pay and had them discharged from hospital.

Giving love and hope is our watchword.

Visitation to Abeni Orphanage home in Benin city, Edo State

On the 19th of May 2018 Amor de dios hope foundation paid a visit to Abeni Orphanage home to offer gift aids to the orphans and management

in line with our aims and objectives to support orphans and the less-privileged gifts aid included food materials, beverages, toiletries and a cash gift of N50, 000 (Fifty thousand naira)

Support & Volunteer

There are a number of ways you can help support the ADDHF. Donate via our Just Giving page, or direct to the charity. Fundraise by organising a local event, individually or involving your community, church or school. You could also take part in a wider fundraising event on the charity’s behalf. Also We need volunteers in the communities where we work to help provide vital services. These include medical care to help vulnerable people, such as the elderly, homeless individuals and orphaned children. We also provide educational support as well as skills training to improve the job prospects of widowed mothers and we provide social care for the elderly and young adults. If you can help us in any way, please contact us.
